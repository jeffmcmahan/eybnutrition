Delicious Healthy Blueberry Bars

Ingredients (makes 6–9 bars):

  • Oats: 1 cup
  • Oat Flour: 1 cup
  • Fresh Blueberries: 1Â½ cups
  • Baking Powder: ¼ tsp
  • Sea Salt: ¼ tsp
  • Cinnamon: ¼ tsp
  • Coconut Sugar: ⅓ cup
  • Unsweetened Apple Sauce: ⅓ cup

Directions (≈95 min.):

  1. Preheat oven to 350°.
  2. Combine dry ingredients.
  3. Mix in applesauce and blueberries.
  4. Pour into 8 × 8 pan.
  5. Bake 30 minutes.
  6. Chill 60 minutes in refrigerator.
