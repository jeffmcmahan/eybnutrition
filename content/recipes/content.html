Healthy Cauliflower Cheddar Soup

Ingredients (makes ≈4 cups):

  • Cauliflower: ¾ head
  • Almond milk: 1¼ cup
  • Shredded sharp cheddar cheese: ¼ cup
  • Vegetable broth: ½ cup
  • Minced garlic: 1 tsp
  • Onion powder: 1 tsp
  • Salt and pepper (see below)

Directions:

  1. Cut cauliflower into individual florets.
  2. Steam florets until soft.
  3. Add all ingredients together in blender.
  4. Blend until smooth.
  5. Salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Sprinkle some parsely flakes and enjoy.

Healthier Chocolate Cheesecake

Ingredients (makes 1 serving):

  • Low fat cream cheese: 2 tbsp
  • Cottage cheese: ⅓ cup
  • Semi-sweet chocolate chips: 2 tbsp
  • Honey: ½ tsp

Directions:

  1. Mix cream cheese, cottage cheese, and honey with a hand mixer until smooth.
  2. Stir in melted chocolate chips.
  3. Chill for at least an hour.

Spicy Coconut Flour Crusted Tilapia

Ingredients (makes 4 servings):

  • Tilapia fillets: 4
  • Coconut flour: 4 tbsp
  • Almond milk: ½ cup
  • Chili powder: 1 tsp
  • Garlic: 1 tsp
  • Sea salt: ½ tsp
  • Pepper: ½ tsp

Directions:

  1. Mix together all dry ingredients.
  2. Slowly add in milk, mixing between pours, until you get a batter consistency (more or less milk may be needed depending on how thick you like the batter).
  3. Coat fillets with batter and place in hot pan.
  4. Cook 5 minutes.
  5. Flip the fillets and cook another 3 minutes or until fully cooked.
