Delicious Healthy Blueberry Bars

Ingredients (makes 6–9 bars):

  • Oats: 1 cup
  • Oat Flour: 1 cup
  • Fresh Blueberries: 1Â½ cups
  • Baking Powder: ¼ tsp
  • Sea Salt: ¼ tsp
  • Cinnamon: ¼ tsp
  • Coconut Sugar: ⅓ cup
  • Unsweetened Apple Sauce: ⅓ cup

Directions (≈95 min.):

  1. Preheat oven to 350°.
  2. Combine dry ingredients.
  3. Mix in applesauce and blueberries.
  4. Pour into 8 × 8 pan.
  5. Bake 30 minutes.
  6. Chill 60 minutes in refrigerator.

Healthy Cauliflower Cheddar Soup

Ingredients (makes ≈4 cups):

  • Cauliflower: ¾ head
  • Almond milk: 1¼ cup
  • Shredded sharp cheddar cheese: ¼ cup
  • Vegetable broth: ½ cup
  • Minced garlic: 1 tsp
  • Onion powder: 1 tsp
  • Salt and pepper (see below)

Directions:

  1. Cut cauliflower into individual florets.
  2. Steam florets until soft.
  3. Add all ingredients together in blender.
  4. Blend until smooth.
  5. Salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Sprinkle some parsely flakes and enjoy.
