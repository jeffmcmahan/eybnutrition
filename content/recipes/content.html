Healthy Cauliflower Cheddar Soup

Ingredients (makes ≈4 cups):

  • Cauliflower: ¾ head
  • Almond milk: 1¼ cup
  • Shredded sharp cheddar cheese: ¼ cup
  • Vegetable broth: ½ cup
  • Minced garlic: 1 tsp
  • Onion powder: 1 tsp
  • Salt and pepper (see below)

Directions:

  1. Cut cauliflower into individual florets.
  2. Steam florets until soft.
  3. Add all ingredients together in blender.
  4. Blend until smooth.
  5. Salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Sprinkle some parsely flakes and enjoy.

Healthier Chocolate Cheesecake

Ingredients (makes 1 serving):

  • Low fat cream cheese: 2 tbsp
  • Cottage cheese: ⅓ cup
  • Semi-sweet chocolate chips: 2 tbsp
  • Honey: ½ tsp

Directions:

  1. Mix cream cheese, cottage cheese, and honey with a hand mixer until smooth.
  2. Stir in melted chocolate chips.
  3. Chill for at least an hour.
